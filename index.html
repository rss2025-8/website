About Us

We are team 8 of the Spring 2025 class "Robotics: Science and Systems (6.4200)" at MIT. On our website, you'll find a collection of videos, briefings, and reports showcasing all the cool things we worked on!

Our labs include a wall follower, a color-based parking controller / line follower, Monte Carlo localization, path planning / following with pure pursuit, and a final challenge featuring a heist in the Stata basement and speed racing around the Johnson track.

Our values are to communicate openly, share responsibilities based on skills and interests, support each other during busy weeks, make decisions together, and stay dedicated to our goals through fair, flexible, and active participation.
